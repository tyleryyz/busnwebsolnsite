Our Sponsor

Project Description

Tailored Tutoring

We are Business-Web Solutions, and our sponsor is Robert Lokken, owner of Tailored Tutoring Co., an online professional tutoring company based here in Flagstaff, AZ.
What sets Robert and Tailored Tutoring Co. apart from other web-based tutoring sites, is the ability for students to take a picture of a problem, upload it to a tutor, and receive a personalized video solution from that tutor. While this feature provides a lot of value to students, it is currently a manual process for our sponsor to complete himself. This process is just too cumbersome to be sustainable long-term without automation.

Problem Statement

Tedious, Manual

Currently, the website is poorly optimized for mobile. Students often find themselves having to take a picture of a problem on a phone, email it to themselves, and then upload it to the Tailored Tutoring website via a computer. Clearly a time consuming process for the student. On Tailored Tutoring’s end, the problem is even worse. Robert gets a notification that a student problem has been submitted, then manually has to go through the submission to see what it is about, and search for a qualified tutor in the subject area that is currently available. He notifies the tutor who then creates a video, uploads it to a Google Drive, and then Robert has to create a personal link to the solution video, and email that to the student, notifying them that their problem has been solved and is viewable. This is obviously a less than ideal process, and could do with some automation and fine-tuning.


Solution Overview

Automated, Streamlined

As the business scales, this whole process is becoming too much work for our sponsor, and needs to be automated. That is where we come in. Our goals here are to completely automate the submission process and improve the site by:
  • Building with mobile in mind by using ReactJS and Bulma which utilize a mobile-first design philosophy.
  • Automating the problem-submission process by building a new web portal. Users and tutors will have profiles, with problems submitted under subjects and automatically sent out to corresponding, available tutors in those subject areas.
  • Incorporating a database to store these personalized tutor-videos for students using MongoDB. From there, students will be able to access their videos for a specified time; while Robert will be able to download to a hard drive and have a physical backup of all videos made.
In conclusion, we are here to help automate Robert and Tailored Tutoring Co.’s business, specifically focusing on their unique feature: the problem-submission process. We will emphasize automation, mobile-friendly design, and a web portal with user profiles to help Tailored Tutoring Co. scale their business into the future.
